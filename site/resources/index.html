Why Pastebot?

It's easy.

Pastebot requires no setup or special permissions. Just invite him to your server, and give him permissions to the channels you want him in.

It's private.

Pastebot never stores your data- not even the pastes it links. These are pulled directly from the CDN. When the message is deleted, the paste is deleted.

It's clean.

Pastebot automatically deletes paste buttons (That's right! Buttons! No messy blue links or massive embeds!) when the triggering message is deleted.

It's fast.

pastebot uses streaming, browser fetch calls, and a robust API to ensure that pastes load quickly, with no tracking or extraneous javascript.

It's free.

Pastebot will always be free to use for 8mb (non-nitro) files. Some larger files unfortunately require payment.

It's quiet.

The bot never advertises, sends random error messages, or adds any spammable commands. It only sends messages when it successfully creates a paste.
